Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa, located in West Africa along the Gulf of Guinea. It is known as the "Giant of Africa" because of its large population (over 220 million people) and strong cultural, economic, and political influence. Nigeria is made up of over 250 ethnic groups, with Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo being the three largest. The country is rich in natural resources, especially oil and gas, and has a diverse cultural heritage expressed through languages, music, art, and festivals. Its capital city is Abuja, while Lagos is the largest city and major economic hub.
